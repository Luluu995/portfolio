My practice sits at the bridge between Western and Eastern design cultures — pairing European craft and editorial rigour with the speed, scale and digital fluency of China's tech industry. The result is work that travels across markets without losing meaning in either.

I trained at the Politecnico di Milano and the Academy of Arts & Design at Tsinghua University in Beijing, and have since built brand worlds, product flows and AIGC systems for clients ranging from global luxury houses to consumer-tech labs and emerging festivals.

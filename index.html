MY APPROACH

Design is a bridge between intent and impact.

I believe design is more than aesthetics. It's strategic, intentional, and rooted in understanding real people. I specialize in creating brand identities and digital experiences that feel authentic, travel across cultural contexts, and drive meaningful outcomes.

Working across Europe and Asia, I've learned that the best ideas don't just look good—they work. They respect local markets while maintaining global vision.

Design for a Connected World

I'm a brand identity and digital strategy designer obsessed with creating work that bridges cultures.

Based between Florence and Shenzhen, I work with ambitious tech companies, luxury brands, and startups to build identities and digital products that resonate globally. My approach combines strategic thinking, cultural intelligence, and a relentless focus on clarity.

I specialize in cross-cultural design—understanding how a brand can maintain coherence while respecting local contexts. Whether it's adapting a European aesthetic for Asian markets or bringing Eastern market insights to Western brands, I see the intersection as opportunity.

Recent focus: AIGC integration into brand design, design systems for scale, and digital strategy for complex B2B products.
